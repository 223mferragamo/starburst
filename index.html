About

Starburst candies are packed with a variety of great tasting fruit flavors and a deliciously chewy texture, to be enjoyed anytime and anywhere. There are many different varieties of Starburst, including Starburst Fruit Chews, Starburst Minis, Starburst Gummies, Starburst GummiBursts® and Starburst Jellybeans.

When was STARBURST created?

STARBURST was created in 1960 in the United Kingdom.

Who created them?

STARBURST is rumored to be created by a man named Peter Pfeffer.

Why was STARBURST invented?

It is beleved that Peter Pfeffer created them for a competition. However, he originally called them "Opal Fruits". They were officially changed to "STARBURST" in 1998.

What flavors do STARBURST come in?

The original STARBURST come in four flavors:
cherry (red), orange (orange), lemon (yellow), and strawberry (pink).
However, other varities of STARBURST have more flavors, such as tropical or duos.

Who owns STARBURST?

STARBURST is owned by The Wrigley Company. They also own many other candies. You can see them all here!
